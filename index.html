bio

about ANJALI

"coast to coast. back to back. evil little grin"

Anjali is a bicoastal indie pop singer-songwriter originally from New Jersey. Her music lives in the emotional space between beauty and ache: introspective, honest, and unafraid of the dark corners of feeling.

With releases like Sociopath, Displacement, and missinu., Anjali builds worlds out of specific moments: the places you try to belong, the person you couldn't stop thinking about, the version of yourself you left behind.

Classically trained in piano from age 5, Anjali brings a deep musicality to everything she creates. She performs solo on the east coast and with her band Anjali & The Unhinged on the west coast, playing originals and covers across Portland and beyond.

connect your calendar

3 steps, takes about 5 minutes

1. get a free Google Calendar API key
Go to console.cloud.google.com → New Project → Enable "Google Calendar API" → Credentials → Create API Key. Copy the key.

2. make your calendar public
In Google Calendar → Settings → your shows calendar → "Access permissions" → check "Make available to public".

3. add your details to the site
In the website code, find the CALENDAR_CONFIG block near the top of the script and paste in your Calendar ID and API key.

after that, just add events to your Google Calendar normally — the site updates within minutes. no code changes ever needed again.
